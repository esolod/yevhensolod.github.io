Why P&L on the order lines

ChartTrader shows you where your Profit Target and Stop Loss sit, but not what either one is actually worth in dollars, points, or ticks — you're left doing the math yourself, or switching to the Positions tab mid-trade.

Order P&L Labels closes that gap by attaching the live figure directly to each working order, calculated from your real position size and average price. It sits next to the ChartTrader line it belongs to, color-matched so it reads as part of the same order, and updates every tick with no repaint.

Typical use: managing size and risk at a glance while a trade is open, without leaving the chart or doing mental math on stop distance.
