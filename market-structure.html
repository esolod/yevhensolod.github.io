What it detects

Minor (internal) structure

Every swing high and low as price forms them — HH, LH, HL, LL — updated candle by candle, no repainting.

Major (swing) structure

Minor structure filters automatically into higher-level swing structure, so you see both the noise and the signal on the same chart.

BOS — Break of Structure

Confirms trend continuation the moment a candle closes beyond the prior swing level.

CHoCH — Change of Character

Confirms a trend reversal the moment a candle closes beyond the opposing swing level.

No ATR, no lookback, no fractals

Structure is derived purely from price — no multiplier or period to tune, no "right" swing size to guess at.

Fully configurable rendering

Independent font, color and line style for Minor structure, Major structure, and unconfirmed Weak Swings.

Why price-action-only structure

Most market structure indicators lean on ATR multipliers or fixed lookback windows to decide what counts as a swing — which means the same chart can show a different structure depending on a setting, and everything repaints as that setting gets tuned.

Market Structure works the other way: a swing high or low is only ever confirmed when a candle closes beyond it. Nothing is inferred from volatility, nothing is assumed from a fixed number of bars. What you see on a closed bar does not change on a later bar.

Typical uses: reading trend direction objectively without discretionary swing-drawing, spotting CHoCH reversals early, and using Minor structure as a faster-reacting layer underneath Major swing context.

FAQ

Does this use ATR or a lookback period?

No. Swing points and BOS/CHoCH are confirmed purely by candle close — no ATR multiplier, no fixed lookback, no fractals to configure.

What's the difference between Minor and Major structure?

Minor is internal structure — every swing as it forms. Major filters that down to swing-level structure. Both run independently and can be toggled and styled separately.

Does it repaint?

No. A swing or a BOS/CHoCH only confirms once a candle closes beyond the relevant level — it does not move or disappear on later bars.
